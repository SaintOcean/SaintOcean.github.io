About

Hi, I am Christine Valencia!

Im a trans woman composer and arranger for film and video games from North County San Diego.

I have always loved video games and the music that goes along with them. I can write in a myriad of different styles.

A good soundtrack can elevate the art and narrative of interative storytelling in incredible ways.

I also have passion for EDM production, score engraving, coding, art, 3d modeling, and design.

I graduated in 2020 with a BA in Electro-Acoustic Music Composition from Chapman University.

There I studied under the late Dr. Dominique Schafer and wrote many chamber works and an opera blending electronic and live performance.

You can find my music online where I post as Saint Ocean.

I would love to work together to create the perfect soundtrack for your game, project, film, orchestra, or performance art.
